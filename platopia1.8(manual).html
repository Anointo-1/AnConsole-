Getting Started

Welcome to the world of PLATOPIA, running on the incredible Anointo Logic Systems Scroller Engine v10.0[cite: 7]! You are about to embark on a thrilling adventure across multiple dangerous biomes. You'll need sharp reflexes and a keen eye to survive the perilous jumps, outsmart the Hunters, and collect the legendary Orbs!

Controller Input

  • D-PAD (Left/Right): Move your hero across the terrain[cite: 5].
  • A BUTTON: Jump! Master this to survive[cite: 5, 7].
  • B BUTTON: Dash into action to take out enemies[cite: 5, 7].
  • START / SELECT: Pause the game or access the Pause Option menu[cite: 5, 7].

Hazards & Items

Keep your eyes peeled for these crucial items scattered throughout the worlds:

  • Orbs: Collect these glowing spheres to increase your score and progression[cite: 7]. Grabbing one creates a burst of blue sparks![cite: 7]
  • Springs: Step on these yellow bouncy pads to get an incredible height boost[cite: 7]. Perfect for reaching those high-up Orbs![cite: 7]
WARNING! Avoid the Lava/Ash Hazards in World 3! They erupt upwards and will instantly cost you a life![cite: 7]

The Worlds of Platopia

Your journey will take you across four distinct and dangerous zones. Make sure you reach the goal area at the far right of the map to survive![cite: 7]

World 1: The Starry Night

A gentle introduction. Jump across basic platforms under a beautiful starry sky and watch out for the simple patrolling ground enemies[cite: 7].

World 2: Industrial Pipes

The challenge ramps up! Navigate across metallic platforms and look out for enemies flying in wave-like patterns[cite: 7].

PRO TIP: There is a Secret Key hidden at the very edge of this map (Coordinates X: 1020, Y: 32)[cite: 7]. Grabbing it unleashes pink sparks and sets the "Secret Unlocked Flag"[cite: 7]!

World 3: Ash & Lava

Things are heating up! This world introduces Slopes and Sturdy Blocks[cite: 7]. You'll also encounter the terrifying "Hunter" enemies here—if they see you, they will get alerted and chase you down![cite: 7]

World 4: Sky City Cyber Theme

The ultimate test of platforming skill. You must navigate sticky horizontal moving platforms, slidable vertical challenges, and fragile crumbling bridges that break if you stand on them too long![cite: 7]
